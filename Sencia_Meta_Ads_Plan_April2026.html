Section 03

Key Problems Identified

Critical issues bleeding budget and inflating CPL

🔴 Problem 1: Andromeda Campaign is a Budget Bleeder

Spending ₹30,839 in 16 days (~₹1,927/day) with CPC of ₹28.27 — nearly 4x higher than Maraimalai Nagar adset. CPM at ₹345 is obscenely high, suggesting audience saturation and poor relevance.

Action: PAUSE immediately. Redirect ₹2,000/day back to performing adsets.

🟡 Problem 2: Budget Misallocation Across Adsets

Maraimalai Nagar adset delivers CPC of ₹7.24 (best) but gets only ₹528/day budget. Meanwhile, 30km Chennai gets ₹1,613/day at ₹15.46 CPC. The best performer is starved.

Action: Reallocate 40% of budget to Maraimalai Nagar. Scale the winner.

🔵 Problem 3: Limited Creative Variety

Only 2-3 active creatives per adset. "Real Estate-365 Rs 2680" carries the load. Creative fatigue is inevitable. Need at least 5-8 creatives per adset for healthy rotation.

Action: Launch 5 new creatives per adset — mix of static, carousel & video.

🟣 Problem 4: No Retargeting Funnel

Running only cold lead gen campaigns. Zero retargeting for website visitors, video viewers, or form openers. Missing easy wins from warm audiences that already know the project.

Action: Set up 3-tier retargeting funnel for warm and hot leads.

🔴 Problem 5: CPL at ₹528 — Nearly 2x Above Target

With 11 leads/day at ₹528 CPL, you're paying ₹5,808/day for leads. At target CPL of ₹325 with the same ₹6,000 budget, you should be getting 18-20 leads/day. The gap is caused by: (1) Andromeda bleeding budget, (2) starving the best-performing adset, (3) no retargeting funnel, and (4) limited creative rotation. This plan fixes all four.

Daily Optimization Playbook

Systematic rules-based optimization to hit ₹300-350 CPL target

🔴 Kill Rules (Pause Immediately)

Ad CPC > ₹25 after 500+ impressions → PAUSE
Ad set CPL > ₹500 after ₹1,500 spend → PAUSE
CTR < 0.5% after 2,000 impressions → PAUSE
Frequency > 2.5 on any ad set → REFRESH CREATIVE
Zero leads after ₹1,000 spend → PAUSE AD

🟢 Scale Rules (Increase Budget)

Ad set CPL < ₹280 for 3 consecutive days → INCREASE 20%
CTR > 1.5% with CPC < ₹12 → SCALE TO 2x BUDGET
Retarget CPL < ₹200 → INCREASE WINDOW TO 30 DAYS
Lookalike CPL < ₹300 → EXPAND TO 2% LOOKALIKE
Testing ad set wins → GRADUATE TO COLD CAMPAIGN

📅 Daily Optimization Checklist

Morning (10 AM):
  • Check yesterday's spend vs budget pacing
  • Review CPL by ad set — flag any above ₹400
  • Check frequency across all ad sets
  • Pause any ads hitting kill rules
Evening (6 PM):
  • Review day's lead count & quality
  • Check if any ad set overspent
  • Scale winners hitting scale rules
  • Plan next day's creative refresh if needed
